Prescriptions

The doctor, after confirming the appointment details would have an option to send the prescription to the patient. This would be done after the doctor has viewed the prediction report/ old reports of the patient based on which he has formed the prescription. The patient would be able to view the prescription once the doctor has sent it.

Response Sequence:
The doctor would view the prediction and old reports of the patient and send him a prescription for the same.
The patient would be able to view the prescription sent by the doctor.
